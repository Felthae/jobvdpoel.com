About Me

Hey, my name is Job van der Poel and I'm a game designer from the Netherlands.

I possess an ever lasting love for video games which caused me to pursue the career of a game designer.

I study at BUAS, Breda University of Applied Sciences, where I follow the International Game Architecture and Design course.

Some of my favorite games include:
The Legend of Zelda series, World of Warcraft and Sea of Thieves.
