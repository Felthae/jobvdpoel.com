Projects

Over the course of several years at IGAD I have worked on a multitude of projects. On this page I will go over these projects and state my contributions.

Compile

During my first year at IGAD I worked as a level designer for Compile. Compile is a first-person free running game where the player has to traverse several obstacles in a level using the movement options available to them. My team and I worked on this game for 14 days during our first year at IGAD. This project was my first opportunity to work on level design.

Unreal Tournament Level Design

During the first block of my second year at IGAD I was tasked with creating an Unreal Tournament Free-For-All level. During this block I spent around 7 weeks to come up with a concept, research themes, build the level in Unreal Engine 4, playtest and iterate upon my level according to feedback received from my playtests. In the end I came up with a gladiator arena themed map with some cool moments and lines of fire.



Game marmalades

During the second block of my second year at IGAD I got teamed up with other students to create a total of 3 games. Each game had a development time of 2 weeks.

Mineblowers

Mineblowers is a time trial type game where players need to get from point A to point B as fast a possible. For this game we developed a custom controller, which was a bike pump. As the player started pumping, the character would start moving. The player would need to pump in order to gain speed, however if they went too fast in the corners, they would get derailed and they would have to start over. For this project I designed the level/track the player has to traverse.



Drawn Home

Drawn Home is a 2D platformer where the player has to combine certain elements from within the environment in order to overcome obstacles. The player is able to transfer themselves in between the real world and a “Drawn” world using a portal, each world had their own look and feel. I worked on the puzzle design for this project.



Steam Punch

Steam Punch is a beat-em-up style game developed for phones. The goal of the game is to defeat all the enemies thrown at the player by either pressing on either the right or the left side of the screen. By doing this the player would charge up a punch in that specific direction and once they released the screen it would send the player character in that specific direction, hitting all enemies along the way. I was the project manager for this project. I would set up a scrum board, make a planning and make sure the project was on track to being finished on time.
