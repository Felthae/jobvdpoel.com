PROJECT INFORMATION

Genre: Fighting game
Team size: 18

Platform: Steam
Status: Released

DESCRIPTION

Steam Link: https://store.steampowered.com/app/1105560/KraKen/

Fight intense 1-on-1 battles with your friends! Kra-Ken is a 2.5D fighting game that will make you grip your controller ever more tightly as you beat your opponent.

RESPONSIBILITIES / MY CONTRIBUTION

  • I applied most of the hurt- and hitboxes to the attacks and moves of both of our characters.
  • I oversaw the hurt- and hitbox process done by peers and provided feedback to their work in order to establish consistency.
  • I worked closely together with the animators in order to secure quality, clarity and accuracy for each of the moves
  • I was responsible for balancing our characters’ damage, movement and other data together with a peer.
  • Working together with the environmental artists in order to establish clarity in the available levels.
  • During early development I was responsible for level design and creating level elements and hazards

Early development

Kra-ken started out as a platform fighter. During this time in development I was responsible for the level design and creating level elements and hazards using Unreal Engine Blueprints. We decided to shift to the current version of the game because we found that the original concept wasn’t fun enough.
